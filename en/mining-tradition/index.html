Mining Tradition in Abercrave

Remembering the industry that shaped the valley.

Coal mining shaped the landscape, economy, and identity of Abercrave. Generations worked in and around the mines, building strong networks of support that led directly to the creation of welfare halls.

This page will grow with new stories, photographs, and memories shared by local families. If you have archives to contribute, please get in touch.
